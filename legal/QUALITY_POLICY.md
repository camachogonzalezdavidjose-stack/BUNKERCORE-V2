# BunkerCore-V2 Quality Policy

We are committed to maintaining a standard of excellence in the provision of authentication services:

## 1. Service Availability
We strive to maintain 99.9% uptime for our authentication services.

## 2. Continuous Security
The BunkerCore-V2 system undergoes periodic security updates. Reported vulnerabilities are addressed under a rapid response protocol.

## 3. Error Management
- **Reporting**: Users may report failures through the official channel.
- **Resolution**: We are committed to investigating and, if necessary, correcting critical bugs within a reasonable timeframe after bug confirmation.

## 4. Evolution
The system architecture is designed to scale efficiently, ensuring that performance does not degrade as the user base grows.
