# Privacy Policy of BunkerCore-V2

**Last updated:** June 14, 2026

BunkerCore-V2 is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information on our platform.

## 1. Data we DO NOT collect
BunkerCore-V2 is built on a "Privacy-by-Design" model. **We do not store biometric data** (fingerprints, facial recognition) on our servers. Biometric authentication occurs exclusively on the user's device (via WebAuthn).

## 2. Data we collect
We only store information necessary for account management:
- Cryptographic identifiers (Public credentials).
- Email address for user identification.
- Connection metadata (Device ID, registration date).

## 3. Data Security
All information stored in our database is protected using AES-256-CBC encryption. Access to data is strictly limited to services required for authentication.

## 4. User Rights
Users have the right to request the deletion of their data at any time by sending a request to our technical support. Once deleted, information is permanently purged from our servers.

## 5. Contact
For privacy-related inquiries, contact the developer through official channels.
